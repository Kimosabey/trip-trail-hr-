Help & Instructions

For completing and processing your expense report (RANGSONS LLP).

gavelProcessing rules

  1. Pay for all business expenses in cash. Do not charge expenses to the company by signing bills unless authorized by the appropriate authority.
  2. Entertainment expenses are permissible only if you are authorized and they directly relate to company business, immediately preceding or following a substantial business discussion.
  3. Obtain receipts for all expenses and attach them. Any expense without documentary evidence must be detailed in full on the report.
  4. Submit within 3 days of returning to headquarters.
  5. If a balance is owed to the company, repay the cashier immediately after your journey and certify it in the report.
  6. Show local conveyance in the specified section, cross-referenced to the travel report.
